Cookies Policy

At Beautiful Gardens, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how we use cookies on our website. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, the types of cookies used, and your choices regarding cookie management.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit them. These files store information about your preferences, sessions, and interactions to help improve your user experience and enable website functionality.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to make our website work more efficiently and understand how visitors interact with it. Specifically, cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings (such as language or region)
  • Enable essential website features like login and form submissions
  • Analyze website traffic and usage trends to improve performance
  • Deliver personalized content and relevant advertisements
  • Keep our website secure from potential threats and fraud

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. Without them, you may experience limited access to certain features.

Performance Cookies: These allow us to collect anonymous information on how visitors use the site, such as which pages are visited most often and any error messages encountered.

Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember choices you make, like language preferences or region, providing a more personalized browsing experience.

Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. These may be set by third-party advertising networks.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third parties who provide services like analytics or advertising on our behalf. These third-party cookies are governed by their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Managing and Controlling Cookies

You have control over whether and how cookies are stored on your device. Most browsers allow you to accept, reject, or delete cookies via settings menus. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Browser Settings: You can configure your browser to block all cookies, allow only certain types, or alert you when a cookie is being set.
  • Cookie Consent Tools: Our website may display cookie consent banners that allow you to customize your preferences at any time.
  • Deleting Cookies: You can clear cookies stored on your device through browser options or settings on your device itself.

Please note that disabling or deleting cookies might affect your user experience, including some features not working as intended.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Your use of cookies is part of your overall online privacy, and you have rights under applicable data protection laws to access, modify, or delete the information that may be stored via cookies. To learn more about how we handle your personal data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

7. Updates to This Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.
